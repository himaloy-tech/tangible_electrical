About Us

We are very pleased to introduce ourselves as a Repairer and Service provider of TRANSFORMER , MOTOR AND SWITCHGEAR . WE have started our journey in 2010 as “IP ENTERPRISE”. During this prolonged time, we have repaired so many Transformers and provided Service also. Now we have formed this Partnership firm and started Transformer, Motors ( A C & D C ) and Switchgear Repairing and Servicing dedicatedly. Side by side we manufacture also (Transformer). After completion of Our Education, we have started our career with a Transformer Company and finished with an another Reputed Transformer Company, after 25 Years. During this long journey, we have gathered a vast experience on FURNACE DUTY TRANSFORMER such as Induction furnace transformer up to 24 pulse, LRF, ARC Furnace duty Transformer, Power Transformer up to 132 KV, Solar Transformer, Earthing / Zig-Zag Transformer, Dry type Transformer Etc. Now we have a modern and equipped organization with all facilities under single roof. We have experienced workers and Engineers to overcome all sorts of problems. We have confidence to satisfy our client, if we get a chance to serve you.
